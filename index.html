Demon Lord Class DnD

Class Features

Hit Dice

1d10 per Demon Lord level

Hit Points at 1st Level

10 + your Constitution modifier

Hit Points at Higher Levels

1d10 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per Demon Lord level after 1st

Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor, medium armor

Weapons: Simple weapons, finesse weapons (including katana)

Tools: None

Saving Throws: Dexterity, Charisma

Skills: Choose three from Arcana, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion, and Stealth

Equipment

- A katana (finesse weapon)

- An arcane focus

- An explorer's pack

- Leather armor

Class Features by Level

Level 1: Aura of Authority

You emanate an aura that demands respect. You gain proficiency in Persuasion and Intimidation skills.

Level 2: Arcane Manipulation

You can manipulate magic to suit your needs. You gain the ability to cast spells from the Sorcerer spell list.

Spellcasting:

Cantrips: 3

Spells Known: 4

Spell Slots: 2 (1st level)

Level 3: Predatory Strike

Once per session, you can unleash a powerful strike with your katana. As an action, you can make a single melee attack with your katana. On a hit, this attack deals an additional 5d10 slashing damage.

Level 4: Ability Score Improvement

You can increase one ability score by 2 or two ability scores by 1.

Level 5: Dual Strike

You can attack twice with your katana whenever you take the Attack action on your turn. You can use this ability twice per fight.

Level 6: Elemental Immunity

You gain immunity to poison damage and the poisoned condition, as well as immunity to non-magical slashing, piercing, and bludgeoning damage.

Level 7: Spell Dodge

You can use your reaction to dodge a spell or attack. When you do so, you gain advantage on the saving throw or impose disadvantage on the attack roll. You can use this ability twice per fight.

Level 8: Ability Score Improvement | Supreme Strike

You can increase one ability score by 2 or two ability scores by 1. (Supreme strike) Once every fight , you can unleash a powerful spell attack. As an action, you can force a single target within 60 feet to make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 5d20 force damage. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage.

Level 9: Arcane Shield

You can create a magical barrier around yourself. As a reaction when you are hit by an attack, you can add +5 to your AC until the end of your next turn. You can use this ability once per long fight.

Level 10: Demonic Resilience

You gain resistance to fire and necrotic damage.

Level 11: True Sight

You gain the ability to see through illusions and invisibility, granting you truesight out to a range of 60 feet.

Level 12: Ability Score Improvement | Greater Spell Mastery | Aura of Fear | Demonic Presence

You can increase one ability score by 2 or two ability scores by 1. You can cast a spell you know without expending a spell slot. Choose one 3st-level spell from the Sorcerer spell list. You can cast that spell at its lowest level without expending a spell slot. You can change the chosen spell when you finish a long rest. As an action, you can force each creature of your choice that you can see within 30 feet of you to make a Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you for 1 minute. You can use this feature once per short or long rest. Your presence is overwhelming. You gain proficiency in Charisma saving throws.

Level 13: No New Features

No new features gained at this level.

Level 14: No New Features

No new features gained at this level.

Level 15: No New Features

No new features gained at this level.

Level 16: Ability Score Improvement

You can increase one ability score by 2 or two ability scores by 1.

Level 17: Arcane Overload

You can push your magical abilities to their limit. Once per long rest, you can cast any spell you know at one level higher than the highest spell slot you can currently cast, without expending a spell slot.

Level 18: Absolute Defense

You gain immunity to all damage types except force and psychic.

Level 19: Ability Score Improvement

You can increase one ability score by 2 or two ability scores by 1.

Level 20: Demonic Overlord

Once per long rest, you can unleash a devastating attack. As an action, you can force all creatures of your choice within a 60-foot radius to make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, creatures take 20d10 force damage and are stunned for 1 minute. On a successful save, they take half as much damage and are not stunned.
